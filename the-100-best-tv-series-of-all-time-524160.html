We have prepared a definitive list of the 100 best TV shows of all time.

  • Game of Thrones (2011 — 2019) Genre: Action, Adventure, Drama
    This TV series is about powerful families who fight for the Seven Kingdoms. There are some conflicts between the kingdoms of men happening, and that’s when an enemy comes into play to threaten them all.
  • Friends (1994 — 2004) Genre: Comedy, Romance
    This may be one of the best series of all time, and it is about 6 friends who go through life experiences together.
  • Breaking Bad (2008 — 2013) Genre: Crime, Drama, Thriller
  • Sherlock (2010 — 2017) Genre: Crime, Drama, Mystery
  • The Big Bang Theory (2007 — 2019) Genre: Comedy, Romance
  • The Wire (2002 — 2008) Genre: Drama, History
    This series is about the streets of Baltimore and how people influence and inhabit their world, including politicians and the media.
  • The Sopranos (1999 — 2007) Genre: Crime, Drama
  • Fargo (2014 — ...) Genre: Crime, Drama, Thriller
  • Prison Break (2005 — 2017) Genre: Action, Crime, Drama
