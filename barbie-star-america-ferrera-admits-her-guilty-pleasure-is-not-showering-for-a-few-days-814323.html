Barbie Star America Ferrera Admits Her Guilty Pleasure Is Not Showering for a Few Days

 Posted on May 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  327 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Warner Bros’ movie Barbie featured an impressive cast, with America Ferrera who joined as a co-star alongside Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling. In 2017, Time magazine named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world. Recently the star has disclosed a curious detail about what she enjoys.

America Ferrera revealed her surprising guilty pleasure.

Vianney Le Caer/Invision/East News, MICHAEL TRAN/AFP/East News

Barbie star America Ferrera made a candid confession during a video interview. As part of a game to test their knowledge of each other’s lives, the film’s director, Greta Gerwig, and co-stars Margot Robbie, Kate McKinnon, and Issa Rae were asked to guess Ferrera’s secret guilty pleasure.

Amidst various food-related guesses, Gerwig eventually suggested that it wasn’t food-related at all. Ferrera answered: “No, I don’t feel guilty about any of those things, but my guilty pleasure would be... I’m going to really regret saying this... It’s not showering for a few days.” Such a candid answer made her colleagues shocked.

Ferrera is not the only star who doesn’t like showering.

America Ferrera is not the first celebrity to be open about her bathing habits. In 2021, Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis revealed that they don’t bathe their children every day. Kunis humorously stated, “Now, here’s the thing: If you can see the dirt on them, clean them. Otherwise, there’s no point.”

Kristen Bell also admitted that she waits until her daughters have a noticeable odor before washing them. Jake Gyllenhaal shared that he doesn’t bathe every day, as it helps with his skin maintenance.

As per scientists, there is no ideal frequency for showering.

According to experts, there is no perfect shower routine for everyone. For most people, showering several times per week is considered sufficient, unless they are particularly grimy, sweaty, or have other reasons to shower more frequently. Short showers lasting three or four minutes, with a focus on cleaning the armpits and groin, may be enough to maintain good hygiene. Ultimately, individual needs and preferences should be taken into account when determining the appropriate shower routine.

By the way, if you have already watched Barbie, you may have noticed that pink dreamhouse from the movie. The good news is that it’s available for rent, but only for two nights. Don’t miss the chance!
