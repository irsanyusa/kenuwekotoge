Elon Musks Doppelgnger Is Training to Fight Mark Zuckerberg

 Posted on September 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  140 words  |  Larita Shotwell

In a recent video, Elon Musk’s doppelgänger showed off his skills during a “training” video just in case he needed to replace the real Elon during the cage fight with Mark Zuckerberg.

If you somehow missed it, the SpaceX boss challenged Zuckerberg to a cage fight. To which Facebook’s CEO left an excellent and short answer: “Send me the location.”

After that, pictures showed up online of Zuckerberg looking strong and ripped after he already started training with UFC fighters.

As a response, Yilong Ma the Chinese lookalike of billionaire Elon-Musk also began posting so-called “training” videos of himself pretending to fight Zuckerberg.

As we all expected, in his version of the “training,” there is no other winner except him. In each of his videos, he funnily wins by one punch K.O.’s.

Of course, he doesn’t depict real fights, only funny mocking movements to amuse the large number of followers he gathered over the years.

The proposition of a fist-off emerged as the result of a disagreement between the 2 prominent rivals, with Musk directly challenging Zuckerberg to face him in this unique contest.
