Sofa Vergara Posts an Extra Cheeky Thong Pic, and People Are Wondering About One Thing

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Sofía Vergara is proving that at 50, a woman can look even more appealing than in her younger years. The star’s recent daring post has taken the internet by storm. And everybody who sees her age-defying looks can’t help but ask this one question.

She’s a Summer girl.

The America’s Got Talent judge shared with her almost 30 million Instagram followers her love for the summer season. She wrote in her native Spanish language a caption that translates to ’’my thing is summer.’’ She added a couple of heart-shaped eyes and sun emojis.

The photo that accompanied her words showed her tanning on her stomach beside a shimmering pool in her backyard, wearing solely a black thong bikini bottom. Vergara elegantly adorned her long and flowing hair while gracefully presenting a silver mirrored compact from her recently launched sun care collection.

Fans and other stars were quick to shower her post with likes and positive comments.
Her fellow America’s Got Talent judge Howie Mandel wrote, ’’Beautiful smile’’, while Sharon Stone left flame emojis.

Countless commentators were praising her figure which looks fabulous. One person said, ’’She’s still got it.’’ Another wrote in Spanish what translates to ’’The years don’t pass by for you, beautiful woman.’’

People can’t believe she’s 50.

Sofía doesn’t shy away from displaying her toned body on social media. And just a few months ago, another jaw-dropping post of the actress has set pulses racing.

That photo showed Vergara taking a mirror selfie as she rocked a black bikini which perfectly flaunted her famous curves.

Her age-defying looks has left no one indifferent and most people were wondering how a person can look this good at 50 with comments such as ’’Almost thought it was a throwback!’’ and ’’Do you ever age?’’

Other comments read, ’’How old are you again — 24?’’ and ’’She’s hot for a 20-year-old, and she’s 50? No frigging way.’’

Sofía Vergara is not just a gorgeous person on the outside, she is also a beautiful person on the inside. And all the things she did in order to help people battle cancer serve as a great proof that she has a pure soul.
