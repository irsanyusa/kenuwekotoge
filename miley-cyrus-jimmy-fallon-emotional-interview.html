Miley Cyrus stopped by The Tonight Show Thursday, where Jimmy Fallon recruited her for his “emotional interview” segment. Basically, this requires both participants to work several different emotions into the conversation as seamlessly as possible. (You may recall Amy Schumer excelling at this a few months ago.)

Fallon and Cyrus take on emotions ranging from “super defensive” to “fake polite,” and they both really succeed at the former. And at one point, Fallon insists Miley’s name is Morley, prompting lots of anger and frustration.
