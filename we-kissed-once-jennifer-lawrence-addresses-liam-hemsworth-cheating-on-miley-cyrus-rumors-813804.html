We Kissed Once, Jennifer Lawrence Addresses Liam Hemsworth Cheating on Miley Cyrus Rumors

 Posted on July 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  354 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Jennifer Lawrence found herself at the center of speculation when rumors circulated about her alleged involvement with Liam Hemsworth while he was still in a relationship with Miley Cyrus. Fans speculated that Miley’s music video for “Flowers” contained references to Lawrence, especially considering their shared connection to Hemsworth and the similarity between Miley’s dress in the video and the one Lawrence wore alongside Hemsworth in 2012. The actress is finally opening up about the situation.

Jennifer Lawrence denies cheating rumors.

AFP/EAST NEWS

During an episode of a talk show with Andy Cohen, Lawrence addressed the cheating rumors surrounding her and Hemsworth. When asked about Miley Cyrus’ music video for “Flowers,” which some believed contained subtle jabs at Lawrence, the actress quickly dismissed any claims of infidelity. She firmly stated that the rumors were “not true” and that the notion of a secret fling with Hemsworth was a total rumor.

“We kissed once”, she admitted.

While Lawrence acknowledged sharing a moment with Hemsworth, she emphasized that it occurred years after Hemsworth and Cyrus had already broken up. The actress clarified that their interaction was a simple kiss, not an indication of a romantic relationship. Given their long history and work on the “Hunger Games” films, she described her relationship with Hemsworth as a friendship.

She called Liam hot in an interview in 2015.

AP/East News

After the cheating rumors emerged, an old interview with JLaw surfaced in which she appears to gush about Liam Hemsworth, revealing the two shared a kiss. “Liam and I grew up together...Liam’s real hot. What would you have done?” she said about her then co-star.

Miley previously addressed the cheating rumors.

ANGELA WEISS/AFP/East News

Miley previously addressed the rumors about cheating herself when her song “Flowers” went viral. In a tweet, she clarified, “I can admit to a lot of things but I refuse to admit that my marriage ended because of cheating. Liam and I have been together for a decade. I’ve said it before & it remains true, I love Liam and always will.”

They all have moved on to different partners.

MARCOCCHI GIULIO/SIPA/EAST NEWS

Since their split, Liam Hemsworth and Miley Cyrus have moved on with new partners. Hemsworth is currently in a relationship with Gabriella Brooks, while Cyrus is dating Maxx Morando.

Conversely, Lawrence married Cooke Maroney in 2019 and welcomed their son Cy in 2022. Despite the close bond formed between Lawrence and Hemsworth during their time working on the Hunger Games franchise, their relationship never evolved into a romantic one.
