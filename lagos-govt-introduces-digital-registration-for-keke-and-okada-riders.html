Lagos Govt introduces digital registration for Keke and Okada riders

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  37 words  |  Larita Shotwell

In a bid to address the challenges associated with the operations of commercial tricycles and motorcycles better known as Keke and Okada respectively in Lagos, the State Government is introducing digital registration to regulate transportation services.
